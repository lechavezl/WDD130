eSports, the way to show our passion as gamers

Besides loving video games, all gamers are attentive to the professional leagues of their favorite video games. Yes, that's right, we are talking about eSports. The eSports are the professional video game championships. When a game becomes very famous around the world, big companies sponsor professional teams of that game to compete with other players who are endowed with great skills. For many years, thousands of players have gathered in a public place or from their own homes to watch the best players in the world fight for the title of “Best in the World”. There are many famous video games. But the most common are:

  • MOBA:Dota 2, League of Legends (LOL), Mobile Legends
  • Shooters:Counter Strike Global Offensive (CS:GO), Call of Duty, Valorant
  • Battel Royale:Fortnite, Free Fire
