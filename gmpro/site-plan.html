Overview

Purpose

Video games have been popular for a long time. Every year, we can observe new releases that are better than the previous ones. Since the pandemic started, this kind of entertainment has become more popular and interesting, so the number of video game fans increases considerably every day. For this reason, I want to create a video game website, where people can find information about their favorite video games, recent eSports, new releases, and also guides and tips to become great gamers. In this way, all those gamers who like to show their skills will be aware of everything that happens in this great world.

Audience

People of all ages who like video games, from kids to adults. So a large percentage of people will be my audience. But to be more specific, even though a lot of people like video games, this kind of entertainment is more popular among those who are 12 to 25 years old. This is because they have less responsibilities than other adults, so they can spend more time playing video games, visiting blogs and watching eSports. So they will be the primary audience for my website, where I can provide them with the information they need.

All About Video games

Do you like video games? If you are here, of course you do! For video game lovers, GamePro is the right place where you can find the latest information on the video game world. If you want to know what are the best video games of the moment, we prepare the topics of your interest. If you want to find the content to create your own blog, look no further, believe us, this is the ideal site for you. eSports

eSports, the way to show our passion as gamers Besides loving video games, all gamers are attentive to the professional leagues of their favorite video games. Yes, that's right, we are talking about eSports. The eSports are the professional video game championships. When a game becomes very famous around the world, big companies sponsor professional teams of that game to compete with other players who are endowed with great skills. For many years, thousands of players have gathered in a public place or from their own homes to watch the best players in the world fight for the title of “Best in the World”. There are many famous video games. But the most common are: MOBA: Dota 2, League of Legends (LOL), Mobile Legends Shooters: Counter Strike Global Offensive (CS:GO), Call of Duty, Valorant Battle Royale: Fortnite, Free Fire

Live eSPorts to the fullest from wherever you are.

We know how excited you are to learn all about what's going on in the world of eSports. That's why at GamePro we want to help you find all the information you want about your favorite eSports. Every week you'll have the most relevant data about tournaments, dates, awards, which video games become famous for participating in eSports, and much more. But where can you find all this valuable information? Only at GamePro. We are fans of video games like you, and we know how important it is to be aware of all the wonderful things happening in the digital world. So don't wait any longer and feel like a real gamer.
