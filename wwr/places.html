Places

We carefully chose the best places to make you feel nature in your body. Not only will you enjoy our famous rivers, but also other places where you can camp, take a walk and admire nature. We are ready to make you have a wonderful day. Don't wait any longer and take a look!

Camping Land

In addition to our wonderful rivers, we have a beautiful place to camp with your family or friends. Here you can enjoy nature, breathe fresh air, see beautiful animals and spend pleasant moments with your family or friends. You can have a picnic, play some outdoor games or anything else you can think of. Ready to enjoy what this world has to offer? We are waiting for you!

Rivers

The main attraction you will find in Super Rafting is our big and fabulous rivers. We have not one option, but three creations of nature that will give you a wonderful experience.

Sleeping River is ideal for those who don't need so much adrenaline. Its tranquility and smooth movement will make you enjoy nature in every moment.

Misty Channel is faster than the previous one and provides more exciting movements if you want to scream with excitement.

Finally, for those who want an experience full of adrenaline will love to go to Turbulent Water. Here you will have excitement to the limit, laughs and unforgettable experiences. Visit us and get to know our famous rivers.
